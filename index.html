About Imagine Dragons

Imagine Dragons is an American pop rock band formed in Las Vegas in 2008. Known for their energetic performances and genre-blending music, they have sold millions of records worldwide and received multiple Grammy nominations. Their music often explores themes of resilience, hope, and human emotion, resonating with a broad audience.

Night Visions (2012)

Imagine Dragons debut album that launched them into international stardom. Blending alternative rock with electronic sounds, Night Visions features hits like “Radioactive” and “Demons.” It's an energetic and emotional record that marked a new era in modern rock.

Smoke + Mirrors (2015)

Their sophomore album dives into deeper, introspective themes, combining electronic textures with poetic lyrics. Smoke + Mirrors showcases a band evolving, delivering intense and heartfelt tracks.

Listen on Spotify:

Evolve (2017)

Evolve introduces a more pop-oriented and danceable sound, reflecting the band's growth. With anthems like “Believer” and “Thunder,” this album celebrates overcoming challenges and embracing constant change.

Listen on Spotify:

Origins (2018)

A diverse collection mixing influences across genres, Origins reflects the band's roots and versatility. Songs explore themes of hope, love, and self-identity.

Listen on Spotify:

Mercury – Acts 1 & 2 (2022)

This ambitious double album delves into raw emotions—love, loss, and vulnerability—paired with sophisticated musical production. It highlights the band's artistic maturity and storytelling depth.

Listen on Spotify:

Loom (2024)

The latest release, Loom, presents a fresh approach with electronic elements and futuristic themes, while preserving Imagine Dragons’ unique essence.
